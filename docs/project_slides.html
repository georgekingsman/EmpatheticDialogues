Chain-of-Empathy: 5-Stage Reasoning

Inspired by Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) ‚Äî mirrors how humans develop empathetic responses.

1
Scenario Understanding
Comprehend the conversation context
2
Emotion Recognition
Identify user's emotional state
3
Cause Inference
Understand why they feel this way
4
Goal Setting
Set therapeutic response objectives
5
Response Planning
Formulate empathetic reply

Fusion Mechanism

Empathy reasoning is fused into GPT-2 via additive injection:

mean_pool(hidden_states) ‚Üí 5 stages
fuse(emotion, scenario) ‚Üí Linear(2H, H)
response + fused ‚Üí residual connection
broadcast-add ‚Üí lm_head ‚Üí logits

Design Rationale

  • Additive fusion over cross-attention: parameter-efficient at 124M scale
  • No activation in Stage 5: preserve positive & negative signals
  • Xavier Uniform init: stable gradients across the chain
